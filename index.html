MCKC High school, Kollegala

Welcome to MCKC High School

The mission of providing quality education, anchoring youth on cultural values,
and patriotism is achieved through multidimensional activities of the Education Foundation.
Creating provisions of world quality educational streams have proved effective.

AIDED MCKC HS KOLLGEAL was established in 1960 and it is managed by the Pvt. Aided.
It is located in the Urban area.
It is located in the KOLLEGALA block of the CHAMARAJANAGARA district of Karnataka.

The school consists of Grades from 8 to 10. The school is Co-educational and
it does have an attached pre-primary school Geetha primary school

All the classrooms are in excellent condition

The school has a library and has 3784 books in its library

The school is Provided and Prepared in School Premises providing a mid-day meal
